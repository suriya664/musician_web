Cardiology Department

Expert heart care and cardiovascular treatment services

Overview

Our Cardiology Department is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by experienced cardiologists dedicated to providing comprehensive heart care. We offer a full range of diagnostic, preventive, and therapeutic services for all types of cardiovascular conditions.

From routine check-ups to complex cardiac procedures, our team ensures the highest standard of care for patients with heart-related issues.

Services Offered

Cardiac Testing

Comprehensive cardiac diagnostic tests including ECG, stress tests, and echocardiography.

Preventive Care

Risk assessment and preventive strategies to maintain heart health.

Cardiac Surgery

Advanced surgical procedures for complex cardiac conditions.
